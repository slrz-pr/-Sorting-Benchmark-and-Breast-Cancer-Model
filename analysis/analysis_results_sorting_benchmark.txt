# Sorting Benchmark Results
Comparison of sorting 1,000 random integers.

- Quick Sort (O(n log n)): 0.00318 seconds
- Bubble Sort (O(n^2)): 0.08895 seconds

Note: Quick Sort was significantly faster. In bioinformatics, algorithms like Quick Sort are essential 
because genomic datasets contain millions of entries where O(n^2) would be computationally prohibitive.
